# Energy CRM — Product Site

A single-page **product website** for **Energy CRM**, the asset-management & automated-trading platform for renewable energy producers by **PROGEMISOFT**.

> **One platform. From asset control to market profit.**
> Energy CRM controls your parks and batteries, sells automatically on the day-ahead and intraday markets, and stays connected to the balancing mechanism — turning every forecast into revenue and keeping you out of imbalance costs, 24/7.

## Pages

| File | Language |
|------|----------|
| [`product-ro.html`](product-ro.html) | 🇷🇴 Romanian |
| [`product-en.html`](product-en.html) | 🇬🇧 English |
| [`index.html`](index.html) | Auto-redirect (RO default, EN for non-RO browsers) |

The RO/EN switch in the top-right nav links the two pages together.

## What's inside

- **Hero** with a live-style operations console
- **Connected markets** — OPCOM, BRM, EPEX SPOT, Nord Pool, IBEX, SEEPEX (real logos)
- **The journey of one MWh** — a scroll-driven story (asset control → forecast → day-ahead → intraday → balancing → profit), each stage shown with a bespoke visual that mirrors the real platform screens
- **Capabilities explorer** — an interactive deep-dive into each capability with selling points and a mini app visual
- **Imbalance-cost calculator** — interactive estimate of yearly imbalance exposure and savings
- Use cases, an "asset-first vs. trading-only" comparison, technical integrations & security, and a CTA

## Tech

- Plain **HTML / CSS / JavaScript** — no build step, no dependencies to install.
- Fonts via Google Fonts; icons via [Remix Icon](https://remixicon.com/) (CDN).
- Fully self-contained: only the local `assets/` folder (logos) is required.

Just open `index.html` (or either product page) in a browser.

## Note

The figures shown in the live console, the journey visuals and the calculator are **demo data, for illustration only**.
