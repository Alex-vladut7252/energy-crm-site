01 · Asset control

It all starts with your asset

You monitor and command your parks and batteries directly through SCADA, in real time. You don't just forecast output: you control it.

  • Power, voltages and daily energy, live (~10s)
  • Battery (BESS) control: SoC, charge/discharge via setpoint
  • Output scheduling across 96 intervals of 15 minutes
The differentiator: real physical control, not just trading
02 · Forecast

The best forecast becomes the plan

The platform pulls in forecasts from multiple sources, automatically compares them by accuracy and picks the best one — the basis of every offer and every order.

  • Multiple weather sources, compatible with the client's own
  • 96 intervals of 15 min/day, with accuracy history
  • Full audit trail of decisions
The foundation of the entire chain
03 · Day-ahead

Sold on tomorrow's market — automatically

From the forecasts, the platform generates the DAM offer, validates it and submits it to OPCOM via API, before 12:00 CET. No manual entry, no missed deadline.

  • Automatic aggregation across all the company's parks
  • Automatic pipeline: draft → submitted → accepted
  • Results and clearing prices, ready to report
12:00 CET deadline, met automatically
04 · Intraday

Adjusted in real time, hands-free

As the forecast shifts, the auto-balancer automatically places orders on the intraday market to keep your position correct — with one-click execution whenever you want to act manually.

  • Active auto-balancer, orders placed based on deviation
  • Live orderbook via WebSocket, hourly & quarter-hourly contracts
  • Complete ledger of thousands of processed orders
Works 24/7 — at night and on weekends too
05 · Balancing

In balance with the system

Connected to the balancing mechanism, the platform automatically notifies the responsible party (BRP) and keeps the position balanced — so that imbalances, and their costs, are kept to a minimum.

  • Scheduled BRP notifications, with templates and confirmation
  • Bilateral contracts (OTC) derived automatically from DAM offers
  • Position balanced automatically → minimal imbalance costs
Imbalance avoidance, by design
06 · Profit

Every MWh, monetized

At the end, you see exactly what you earned: DAM and IDM revenue and imbalance costs, in a single report — with export for accounting and management.

  • Market breakdown: Day-Ahead vs. Intraday (revenue, net)
  • The complete transaction ledger, exportable
  • Financial and accuracy reports, over flexible periods
Maximum profit per MWh
